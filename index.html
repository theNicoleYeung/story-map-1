the Green Heart of Europe

The Green Heart of Europe was brought together in 2013 to highlight and protect distinct, spectacular ecosystems across 12 countries in eastern and central Europe.

From the last-remaining intact rivers and wetlands, to ancient primary
old-growth forests, to the last great wilderness areas, the Green Heart represents hope and sanctuary for countless unique, endangered flora and fauna.
